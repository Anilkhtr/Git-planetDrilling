About US

Planet Drilling Company is a licensed and bonded water well drilling contractor in Nepal. For more than 6 years, our company has drilled water wells for homeowners, as well as for business purpose and commercial users. We specialize in well construction, and are dedicated to being the best in our specialty.
Our principal objective is to acheive maximum success in the field of water well drilling area in deep as well as shallow tube-well in rocks formation area.
What sets us apart from our competitors is the quality of work we provide for all of our jobs. We are committed to provide high level of customer satisfaction by providing quality output and real value for money. work towards exceeding customer's expectations through commitment to excellence, constantly upgrding the drilling rigs to suit the ever changing market demands and trend..
